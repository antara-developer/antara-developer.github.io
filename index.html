About ACS Rockstars

ACS Rockstars is an annual celebratory event where we honor the best and brightest of ACS GDC. Established in 2016 to inculcate a culture of meritocracy and appreciation, Rockstars is now the most exciting event of the year! Individuals and teams who have delivered exceptional results in the past year are recognized by leaders and peers and rewarded at a grand ceremony. The event is also a platform for team members to come together, showcase their talents, and have a whole lot of fun. The ultimate culmination of stellar work and dedication, ACS Rockstars aims to celebrate the extraordinary in you!

ACS Rockstars 2023

Welcome to ACS Rockstars 2023! This year we are back IRL after an eventful couple of years where we went virtual.

What you can expect: a chance to nominate and vote for your favorite people, the opportunity to showcase your talent(s), high energy performances, glitz and glamour, recognition of our best achievers, and much more!

The event will happen across both our Noida & Bangalore offices and is scheduled for the first week of March. Dates to be announced!
